## Description

i

and

j

in the array such that the

absolute

difference between

nums[i]

and

nums[j]

is at most

t

and the

absolute

difference between

i

and

j

is at most

k

.

Example 1:

Input:

nums =

[1,2,3,1]

, k =

3

, t =

0

Output:

true

Example 2:

Input:

nums =

[1,0,1,1]

, k =

1

, t =

2

Output:

true

Example 3:

Input:

nums =

[1,5,9,1,5,9]

, k =

2

, t =

3

Output:

false
