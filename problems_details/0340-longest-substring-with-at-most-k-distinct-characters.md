## Description

Given a string, find the length of the longest substring T that contains at most

k

distinct characters.

Example 1:

Input:

s =

"eceba"

, k =

2

Output:

3

Explanation:

T is "ece" which its length is 3.

Example 2:

Input:

s =

"aa"

, k =

1

Output:

2

Explanation:

T is "aa" which its length is 2.
